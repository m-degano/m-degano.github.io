About the Workshop

Join us on Wednesday 16 October 2024 for a workshop hosted by the Institute for Logic, Language, and Computation on the occasion of Marco Degano's PhD defense.

The event will be held at Bushuis, Room F1.01B. Everyone is welcome to attend. Online participation is also possible via Zoom (please see Zoom link below).

Details of Marco Degano's PhD defense, scheduled for the day before, Tuesday 15 October 2024, can be found here.

Schedule

09:10 – 09:30 Welcome and Coffee
09:30 – 10:10 Marco Degano (Amsterdam): Indefinites and their values
10:10 – 10:50 Jacopo Romoli (Düsseldorf): Homogeneity and quantifiers
10:50 – 11:10 Break
11:10 – 11:50 Raquel Veiga Busto (Amsterdam): Layering of manual and non-manual markers in Catalan Sign Language indefinites [online]
11:50 – 12:30 Sigrid Beck (Tübingen): Present Day English 'every' and plural readings
12:30 – 14:00 Lunch Break
14:00 – 14:40 Tomasz Klochowicz & Sonia Ramotowska (Amsterdam): Neglect-Zero Effects in the Interpretation of Quantifiers and Disjunction
14:40 – 15:20 Milica Denić (Tel Aviv): QUD branching in implicature computation
15:20 – 15:30 Break
15:30 – 16:10 Aleksi Anttila & Søren Knudstorp (Amsterdam): Convex Team Logics
16:10 – 16:50 Fan Yang (Utrecht): Complete logics for elementary team properties
16:50 – 17:00 Break
17:00 – 17:40 Donka Farkas (UC Santa Cruz): Rhetorical questions revisited

All times listed are in local Amsterdam time (Central European Summer Time; UTC+2).
