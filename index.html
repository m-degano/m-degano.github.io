Welcome!

I am a postdoc at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC) of the University of Amsterdam (UvA), under the supervision of Robert van Rooij.

Before that I did my PhD at the ILLC (2020 - 2024), supervised by Maria Aloni and Floris Roelofsen. I also completed my MSc in Logic at the UvA and my BA in Philosophy at the University of Padua.
