Welcome!

I am a postdoc at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC) of the University of Amsterdam (UvA), under the supervision of Robert van Rooij.

Before that I did my PhD at the ILLC, supervised by Maria Aloni and Floris Roelofsen. I also completed my MSc in Logic at the UvA and my BA in Philosophy at the University of Padua.

My PhD project concerned formal semantics analyses of indefinite pronouns and determiners across languages. My approach was to develop formal semantics frameworks able to capture the complex landscape of indefinites cross-linguistically, integrating their distribution, functional restrictions, and diachronic pathways.
